Green Valley Residences - Amenities and Facilities

Our project offers world-class amenities designed for modern living and comfort.

Sports and Recreation:
- Olympic-sized swimming pool with separate kids pool
- Fully equipped gymnasium with latest equipment
- Indoor badminton court and table tennis
- Basketball court and cricket practice nets
- Jogging and cycling track around the property

Community Spaces:
- 10,000 sq ft clubhouse with party hall
- Multi-purpose hall for events and gatherings
- Library and reading room
- Children's play area with safe equipment
- Meditation and yoga center

Security and Safety:
- 24/7 CCTV surveillance
- Trained security personnel at all entry points
- Biometric access control
- Fire safety systems on all floors
- Emergency power backup for common areas

Convenience:
- High-speed elevators (2 per tower)
- Covered parking for all residents
- Rainwater harvesting system
- Sewage treatment plant
- Waste management and recycling facility
- Wi-Fi enabled common areas

The project is Vastu-compliant and features energy-efficient lighting throughout. All amenities are maintained by professional facility management team.
